7 Layer Texas Dip

Description

A layered dip with a bean base. Serve with tortilla chips.

This is a wonderful game day dish.

Ingredients

  • 9x13 Pan
  • 2 cans jalapeno refried beans (mix with butter)
  • 3 medium avocados
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 pkg. taco seasoning
  • green onions
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 cup grated cheese

Steps

  1. Heat refried beans on stove. Mix in butter.
  2. Transfer beans to 9x13 pan.
  3. Mash avocadoes well in medium bowl. Mix in lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Transfer to pan.
  4. Mix sour cream, mayo, and taco seasoning in medium bowl. Transfer to pan.
  5. Add grated cheese, then tomatoes, then green onions.
